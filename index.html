SF-50 Block 26 Validator

Eligibility Criteria Reference

Based on 5 U.S.C. 2108 and OPM guidelines [2][4][7]:

  • Retired officers O-4+ ineligible unless disabled (30%+) [4]
  • Requires minimum 180-day qualifying service [1]
  • Medical retirees treated differently than regular retirees [4]
  • 30%+ disability grants 10-point preference [3]
